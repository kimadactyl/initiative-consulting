Let us help you take the initiative

Initiative Consulting is Kate and Peter Foale. We can offer a wide range of complimentary skills and experience to enable you or your organisation in the public, private or voluntary sector to move forward. We can help with:

  • School building projects including BSF or PFI
  • Strategic planning and recruitment
  • Developing people potential
  • Research analysis, monitoring and evaluation

Peter Foale

Peter Foale has worked as a freelance educational consultant since December 2003. Prior to that he worked for 16 years for Nottinghamshire County Council. This period included 5 years as PFI project manager with lead responsibility for the planning and delivery of PFI projects.

His overriding aim has always been to enable clients to identify ways in which school building programmes can improve the quality of the learning environment and the opportunities that are available to learners.

Kate Foale

Kate Foale graduated from Warwick University in 1976 and since then has worked in the education, voluntary, public and private sectors. During this time she has developed a strong sense of what makes a successful enterprise. She has real, hands on experience and understanding of how to develop a clear strategy to take your organisation forward.

People are the key to any successful organisation and Kate is an experienced facilitator, able to work with individuals and groups at all levels to develop their potential and capacity for change, working towards a shared vision.
